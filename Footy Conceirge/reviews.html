PUMA Introduces evoPOWER 1.2 Dragon

Puma has taken the dragon and incorporated it into its fabled evoPOWER line.

The glossy red upper is certainly a decent looking color for this evoPOWER and is a much better color than the dark navy seen on the launch evoPOWER 1.2.

A white Puma formstripe finishes up the colours used and makes it stand out. Puma have gone with a blue soleplate which looks amazing in the shade they have used. A design on the heel plays into the dragon theme well with a lighter blue being used for it. It also includes a special Puma logo onto of the design which rounds off the boot nicely.
